Windows 10 End of Life: Is Your Business Ready?

It is the end of an era. Microsoft has announced the official "End of Life" (EOL) for Windows 10. This means no more security updates, no more patches, and no more support.

For a home user, this is annoying. For a business in Ramsey or Paramus, this is a critical security risk.

Why You Can't Just Keep Using It

Once Microsoft stops releasing security patches, any new vulnerability discovered by hackers will remain open forever. Using Windows 10 after the EOL date is like leaving your office front door unlocked at night.
