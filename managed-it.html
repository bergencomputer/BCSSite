Stop Paying for Downtime.

Many small businesses in Ramsey wait until something breaks to call an IT guy. This "Break-Fix" model is expensive and unpredictable. When your server goes down, your business stops, and you pay emergency rates to get it back up.

Bergen Computer Solutions is different. We utilize a Managed Services model. We install pro-grade monitoring agents on your network that alert us to issues before they cause downtime. We often fix problems while you sleep.

Our Managed Service Package Includes:

  • 24/7 Remote Monitoring: We watch your disk space, CPU usage, and hardware health.
  • Unlimited Help Desk: Your employees can call our local NJ number for instant remote support.
  • Vendor Liaison: Internet down? We sit on hold with Verizon or Optimum so you don't have to.
  • Patch Management: We whitelist and install Windows updates to ensure security compliance.
  • On-Site Visits: If we can't fix it remotely, we dispatch a technician from our Main St. office immediately.
